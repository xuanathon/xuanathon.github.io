About Me

Who am I?

I'm Xuan (pronounced like Schwann cells, for all you biology fans out there)

I don't have a formal background in computer science nor design. Instead, I studied psychology for my undergraduate degree because I am passionate about building empathy with others. I think technology is definitely an area in which there could be more of that. So, I decided to pursue a Master's degree studying UX design at the University of Michigan School of Information.

I believe that in order to achieve a really great design, we must first take the time to understand the experiences, behaviors, and emotions that drive our users. As a current UX student, I aim to further develop my skills as a designer and coder. I look for opportunities to grow not only as an experience designer, but also as a creative thinker and effective communicator.

If I had to summarize myself through a series of images, it would look like...

Though I didn't start out studying computer science, I did eventually learn enough code to get hired as a front end developer.

I have a pretty big sweet tooth, so I learned how to bake. I love finding and experimenting with new recipes.

One of my favorite shows, Adventure Time. I've been a fan of animation as far back as I can remember.

My blue merle corgi, Wilbur. He has the personality of Courage the Cowardly Dog and will play fetch with you until the end of time.

I'm a gamer, and a pretty competitive one at that. I used to play semi-competitively, but am now "retired."

I also love indie games, and Hollow Knight is one of my favorites.

I played the violin for over 13 years and was a member of the Detroit Symphony Youth Orchestra. While I don't play nearly as much anymore, hearing a classical concerto still leaves me nostalgic.

My experiences have given me a deep appreciation for music. I love (almost) all genres!

Sometimes I design things! I love working with digital tools to create illustrations and manipulate images to augment my designs.

Growing up, I loved to draw and adored Photoshop. Now, I am trying to broaden my skillset with graphic and UI design.

Neil Gaiman is one of my absolute favorite authors, and the Sandman graphic novel series is an amazing work of art and literature.

Fashion is another hobby of mine. I love anything that tries to intersect utility with design and see fashion as a sort of "wearable" art.
