About Me

Who am I?


I'm Xuan (pronounced like Schwann cells, for all you biology fans out there). I'm a Michigan native who hates the cold.

Although I don't have a formal background in computer science nor design, I decided to pursue a Master's degree studying UX design at the University of Michigan. Having studied psychology for my undergraduate degree, I am especially passionate about building empathy with others. I believe that in order to achieve a really great design, we must first take the time to understand the experiences, behaviors, and emotions that drive our users.

As a current UX design student, I aim to further develop my skills as a designer and coder. I look for opportunities to grow not only as an experience designer, but also as a creative thinker and effective communicator.
